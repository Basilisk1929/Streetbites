Our Mission

At StreetBites, our mission is simple: to ensure no one goes to bed hungry. We leverage technology and community spirit to create a bridge between excess food and the millions who face food insecurity every day.

How You Can Help

1. Tell Us About Your Food

Click a "Donate" button and fill out a simple form on the next page with your contact info and details about the meal.

2. We Arrange Pickup

Our network of volunteers gets notified. We'll coordinate a convenient time to pick up the donation from your address.

3. You Change a Life

Your donation is delivered directly to a local shelter or community in need, providing a warm meal and hope.

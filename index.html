Our Mission

At StreetBites, our mission is simple: to ensure no one goes to bed hungry. We leverage technology and community spirit to create a bridge between excess food and the millions who face food insecurity every day.

By redirecting surplus meals, we not only feed the hungry but also combat food waste, contributing to a healthier planet.

How You Can Help

Sign Up

Create an account as an individual or an organization. It's quick, easy, and the first step to making a difference.

List Your Donation

Have surplus food? Fill out our simple form with details about the meal, quantity, and when it was prepared.

Change a Life

Our network of volunteers picks up your donation and delivers it to a local shelter or community in need, providing a warm meal and hope.
